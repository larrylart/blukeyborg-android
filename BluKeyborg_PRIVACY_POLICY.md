# BluKeyborg – Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2025-12-01_

BluKeyborg (“the App”) is an offline, on-device utility designed to communicate locally with the user’s own Blue Keyboard Dongle (“the Dongle”).  
The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information to any external servers.

---

## 1. Information We Do Not Collect

BluKeyborg does **not** collect, process, store, or transmit:
- Personal data  
- Contact information  
- Device identifiers  
- Location data  
- Usage analytics  
- Crash reports  
- Advertising identifiers  
- Passwords, credentials, or other sensitive information  

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertisement networks, tracking SDKs, or cloud services.

---

## 2. Local Communication Only (BLE)

BluKeyborg communicates **only** with the user’s own hardware dongle using **Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)**.  
This communication is:
- Local (device ↔ dongle only)  
- Encrypted (micro-TLS handshake)  
- Never accessible to third parties  
- Never sent over the internet  

No BLE identifiers or data packets are collected or shared.

---

## 3. Password & Data Handling

- Passwords are **never** stored by the App.  
- Passwords are **never** logged, uploaded, or transmitted to any server.  
- Passwords are sent **only** over an encrypted BLE session directly to the user’s dongle.  
- The App does not retain any history or persistent logs.

---

## 4. Permissions

BluKeyborg requires Bluetooth permissions to function.  
These permissions are used exclusively to:
- Scan for the user’s dongle  
- Connect to the dongle  
- Securely exchange encrypted commands  

The App does **not** use Bluetooth in the background and does not collect nearby device information.

No other sensitive permissions are required.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App uses **no** third-party:
- SDKs  
- Analytics  
- Error reporting  
- Cloud storage  
- Advertising services  

All operations occur entirely on your device.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

BluKeyborg shares **no data** with:
- Developers  
- Third parties  
- Advertisers  
- Cloud providers  
- Analytics providers  

There is no external communication of any kind.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The App collects no data, contains no tracking, and performs no communication with servers.  
It is safe for all ages, although it is intended for technical users operating the BluKey hardware.
